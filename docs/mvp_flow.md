# Auto-Hub MVP Flow
Version: v0.1 (Manual)

## Overview

This document defines the exact step-by-step process for running AutoHub manually.

Goal:
- find listings
- filter risk
- contact sellers
- find buyers
- connect deals
- learn from real interactions

Rule:
Do not overthink. Follow steps.

---

## Daily Objective

Minimum per day:

- find 5 listings
- validate each listing
- message 5 sellers
- attempt at least 1 buyer interaction

---

## Flow

### Step 1 — Find Listings

Source:
- Facebook Marketplace
- Local FB groups

Criteria:
- price: $3k–$15k
- location: Toronto area
- skip obvious junk

Time limit:
- max 15 minutes

Do not search for perfect deals.

---

### Step 2 — Validate Listing

Use: deal_validation.md

Actions:
- assign risk score
- assign risk level (Low / Medium / High)

Rules:
- skip HIGH risk (for now)
- keep LOW and MEDIUM

Store:
- add to table

---

### Step 3 — Message Seller

Use: scripts.md

Actions:
- send first message
- if reply → continue flow
- if no reply → follow-up later

Goal:
- get permission
- confirm seller is real

---

### Step 4 — Store Listing

Add to table:

- title
- price
- mileage
- link
- notes
- risk
- seller status

Important:
Notes must contain your judgment.

---

### Step 5 — Find Buyer

Sources:
- FB groups
- comments
- people asking “looking for car”

Action:
- send short message
- offer help

Goal:
- start conversation

---

### Step 6 — Understand Buyer

Ask:
- budget
- type of car
- urgency

Do not over-question.

---

### Step 7 — Send Options

Action:
- send 2–3 listings (not more)

Message:
- include your notes
- mention any risks

Goal:
- create trust

---

### Step 8 — Offer Inspection

If buyer interested:

- suggest mechanic check
- offer to help arrange

Goal:
- move toward real action

---

### Step 9 — Connect

If both sides are ready:

- connect buyer ↔ seller
- optionally connect mechanic

You are facilitator.

---

### Step 10 — Record Outcome

After interaction:

- did buyer respond?
- did seller respond?
- was there interest?
- any problems?

Write in notes.md

---

## Rules

- do not build product yet
- do not optimize too early
- do not wait for perfect listings
- do not spam

---

## Success Definition (Early)

Success is NOT:
- revenue
- scale

Success is:
- real conversations
- repeated interactions
- at least 1 serious buyer

---

## Failure Definition

After 5–7 days:

- no replies
- no interest
- no conversations

→ rethink approach

---

## Notes

- speed > perfection
- action > planning
- real users > ideas
